A small experiment in getting better career advice.

We're running a private test with a small group of women. You post a real career question, and within a few days, a handful of women who've navigated something similar will send you a short, candid response in their own words.

No algorithm. No community feed. No formal mentorship commitment. Just real perspectives from women who've been there.

Because you're part of an early test, matching may take longer than we'd like. We appreciate your patience and your willingness to help us figure this out.
