hi M,

what you're going through, i feel, is what many people are going through. it's not easy but you're definitely not alone.

my transition experiences are quite different from yours, so i can't offer any advice on your specific decisions. but i'd like to share what i feel could be helpful as you figure it all out—which you will.

1. your questions are quite near-term focused. and i wonder if you have thought about your career in the long term. what ultimately would you like to be doing? what would you want your work life to feel like? who do you want to be?

often, decisions become much easier when we are clear about our big picture goals. think of them as your personal north-stars.

2. i'm glad to hear that you recognize your needs at a different life stage and aren't willing to compromise your work/life balance. that's important to keep in mind. after all, work is only there to support your happiness in life, not the other way around. never feel bad about having those needs. stand your ground firmly. the right environment will respect it.

3. what you think as the binary decision likely is not as black and white. why don't you just start researching and interviewing? you probably will learn more about the opportunities out there and be more able to judge what's the better direction for you through those experiences.

hope you find those thoughts helpful and i wish you the very best luck with whatever comes next!
